1. Platform Shoes

2. Bell Bottom Pants

A personal favorite of mine, the flare of bell bottom pants were perfect for showing off stylish shoes while strolling in comfort.

3. Crop Tops

A trend that's made a TRIUMPHANT comeback in recent years, these belly-showing tops were stylishly worn with before-mentioned bell bottoms, shorts, or miniskirts. Today, they're often paired with highwaisted jeans.

4. The Farrah Fawcett Haircut

The iconic feathered hairstyle of Charlie's Angel Farrah Fawcett is a staple of her time. Only attempted by the bold, this look required patience and alot of hairspray.

5. The Afro

Black men, women, boys, and girls were all sporting their natural naps and curls with tall, tight afros. Famous examples are James Brown, the brothers of the Jackson 5, and Diana Ross.
